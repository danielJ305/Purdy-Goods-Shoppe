    Wire Mesh Satin Brass Furniture and Creative Grille Mesh

    These fine architectural wire meshes creates an all-purpose weave that will accommodate any application. Improve or create kitchen cabinets, hutches, curios and other furniture or project.

    Our selection of black matte wire meshes and grilles will give a look of distinction to your kitchen or bar cabinets. These architectural meshes have a beautiful satin brass finish like no other.

    This unique twin wire flat top pattern adds visual texture and interest wherever it is used. The wires are crimped in such a way to create facets that collect and redirect light from all angles to allow the mesh to sparkle in its environment. Each side of the mesh has very different textures and either side can be showcased in an application.

    This item is offered in two options. One is a sample size (3-3/4" x 3-3/4") and second is a full sized (40" X 72") sheet. Please order a sample size before placing an order for the large size.
